* Spicy Orange Barbeque Pork

Quick and simple, this dish makes the perfect mid-week dinner. Marinate lean pork steaks in herbs and

spices for an hour, then pop on the barbeque or fry in a pan, and finish with a tangy orange sauce. Just

the right amount of sweetness and spice to give your taste buds a treat.

Ingredients

  1. 2 clove garlic crushed
  2. 2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  3. ½ teaspoon ground coriander (cilantro)
  4. Sea salt and ground black pepper
  5. ½ teaspoon ground paprika
  6. 125ml orange juice freshly squeezed
  7. 4 cups broccoli and cauliflower, steamed
  8. 2 cups low GI rice

Nutrition per serving

  • Energy: 1472Kj (416 calories)
  • Protein: 36.9g
  • Total fat: 11.5g
  • Saturated fat: 2.8g
  • Carbohydrates: 36gFibre: 9g
  • Potassium: 1287mg
